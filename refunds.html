Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

At RoleRover (myrolerover.com), we want you to be completely satisfied with our service. Because our Auto-Apply Engine consumes resources and API calls to process and submit applications, we have established the following Refund Policy.

1. Refund Eligibility

  • Unused Credits: You are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of your purchase, provided that zero (0) job applications have been submitted on your behalf.
  • Used Credits: Once our Auto-Apply Engine submits even a single (1) job application on your behalf, the package is considered partially consumed, and no refunds can be issued.
  • Non-Expiring Credits: Because your credits never expire, you do not need to request a refund due to time constraints; you can save your credits and use them when matching jobs become available.

2. Job Availability & Speed

We process applications within 1 to 30 days depending entirely on active matching roles in the market. We do not issue refunds for slower application rates when they are caused by a lack of relevant job postings in your selected industry/location, as this is out of our control.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for unused credits:

  1. Email billing@myrolerover.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Include your purchase receipt or transaction reference.
  3. Our billing team will verify that no applications have been submitted and process your refund within 3–5 business days.

Refunds will be credited back to your original payment method. If you paid in Naira (NGN), you will receive the exact Naira amount paid. For foreign currency transactions, exchange rate fees or bank conversion differences may apply and are determined by your card issuer.
