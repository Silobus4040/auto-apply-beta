Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI agent tailor my resume?

Our AI analyzes the job description keywords and dynamically highlights your most relevant skills and experience, ensuring you pass the ATS screening perfectly without changing your core facts.

Which job boards and ATS platforms are monitored?

We monitor all major platforms including LinkedIn, Indeed, Greenhouse, Workday, SuccessFactors, Lever, and many more, capturing roles the second they go live.

Is my personal data safe?

Yes, your data security is our top priority. We use secure encryption to store your information and only use it to automatically fill application forms on your behalf.

How many jobs will the AI agent apply to?

Depending on your plan, our Pro Auto-Apply Engine will apply to up to 500 relevant jobs within 1 to 10 days, targeting only fresh openings posted within 24 hours.

Can I review the applications before they are submitted?

You can track every application, optimization, and submission status in real-time directly from your dashboard.

Do my credits expire? What if there aren't many jobs in my country?

Never. Your credits have a lifetime guarantee and never expire. Because our AI scans live employer portals, the number of matches depends entirely on active hiring volume in your target region and role (e.g., US tech roles naturally have higher volume than countries like Nigeria). You are only charged for actual, successful applications.
