✉ Dedicated Auto-Apply Inbox

Why do we need this?
Many enterprise job portals (like Workday and SuccessFactors) require you to create an account and verify your identity with OTP (One-Time Password) codes just to submit an application.

Declutter Your Personal Inbox
Applying to hundreds of jobs means hundreds of automated "Thank you for applying" emails. By generating a secure @myrolerover.com address, we keep all that spam out of your personal email!

How it works:
  • Automated Verifications: Our system automatically catches sign-up verification codes on your behalf to bypass OTP blocks. (OTP codes will not be forwarded to you).
  • Important Emails Only: The only emails we will forward to your personal inbox are real messages from recruiters or hiring managers.
  • Seamless Communication: When a recruiter reaches out, you can reply directly from your personal email app, or right here in the dashboard. Our system masks your address, so the recruiter always sees your professional AI email.
